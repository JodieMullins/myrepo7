Humans have reached Mars

The Starship rocket successfully landed on the red planet this morning.

After a 115 days long journey, the crew of 12 finally arrived at their destination. This marks three firsts in human histoy:

  1. Humans On Mars
  2. Private Spaceship On Mars
  3. Hydroponic Garden Established on Mars
Today we celebrate a monumental step forward in advancing civilization.
